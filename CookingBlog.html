My Cooking Adventures

Easy Weeknight Meals

To save some money (and eat healthier), I've started cooking a lot more. My go-to grocery store is Trader Joe's, but I'll occasionally stop by Whole Foods. Here are a few simple recipes I make all the time.

Greek Chicken Bowls

This is a versatile meal that's great for meal prep. You can customize the toppings based on what you have.

  1. Cook chicken breast and season with oregano, lemon juice, salt, and pepper.
  2. Prepare a base of quinoa or rice.
  3. Top with chopped cucumbers, cherry tomatoes, red onion, and feta cheese.
  4. Drizzle with tzatziki sauce.

Quick Taco Bowls

Faster and often healthier than regular tacos. This comes together in about 20 minutes.

  1. Brown some ground beef or turkey with taco seasoning.
  2. Warm up a can of black beans and some frozen corn.
  3. Serve the meat, beans, and corn over a bed of lettuce or rice.
  4. Add your favorite toppings: salsa, shredded cheese, avocado, or sour cream.
